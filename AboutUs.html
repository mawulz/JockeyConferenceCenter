About JCC

Jockey’s Conference Center building is a versatile and dynamic space designed to host a wide range of
events, gatherings, and conferences. Its impressive architecture and expansive interiors make it an
ideal venue for conventions, trade shows, exhibitions, and other large-scale functions.

History

Our conference center's story dates back decades, beginning as a humble vision of community leaders who recognized the need for a space that could bring people together. It all started with a shared dream of fostering collaboration, knowledge exchange, and economic growth within our city.

Throughout the years, our conference center has hosted a myriad of events that have left lasting impressions. From international conferences and trade shows to cultural festivals and community gatherings, our venue has become a hub for creativity, inspiration, and progress.

Esteemed speakers, industry leaders, and renowned artists have graced our stages, sharing their expertise and captivating audiences from around the world.

Certified

Jockey’s Conference Center is proud to announce that we have passed an in-depth set of assessments to achieve the honor of ISO 14001-2023. The implementation of ISO 14001-2023 allows us to constantly improve our quality management systems and the processes of our organization. This certification shows that we are truly committed to the quality of our ingredients, as part of our business culture and philosophy.
